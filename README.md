# Dream Machines: The History of Artificial Imagination

## Project Overview
Dream Machines is a museum-style website that explores how humans imagined intelligent machines before they fully existed, and how those dreams, fears, and creative ideas shaped the development of artificial intelligence and generative technology.

The goal of this project is to create a modern, unique, and visually memorable museum website that feels like a curated exhibition instead of a generic class project.

## Design Style
I used a Bauhaus-inspired design style with a futuristic digital interpretation.

Bauhaus influenced the layout, structure, spacing, and typography of the site. I chose this style because it creates strong visual order while still feeling modern. The futuristic layer supports the theme of artificial imagination, machine intelligence, and speculative technology.

## Brand Archetype
I chose the Magician archetype.

The Magician archetype fits this project because the site is about transformation, invention, and the power of imagination. It reflects the idea of turning human dreams and abstract ideas into intelligent systems. This archetype influenced the mysterious tone, visual mood, and emotional feel of the website.

## Method of Persuasion
I used the Cialdini principle of Authority.

I used this principle by designing the site like a curated and educational museum exhibit. The goal was to make the site feel trustworthy, thoughtful, and worth exploring. The structured presentation, exhibit tone, and organized sections help encourage visitors to stay engaged with the content.

## AI Orchestration Process
For this project, I used AI as part of a structured workflow instead of asking for one final answer in a single step.

My process followed this pattern:

Spec → QA → Sprint → QA → Implement → QA

First, I defined the concept and direction of the museum site. Then I reviewed the idea, selected the design style, persuasion principle, and archetype, and broke the work into smaller parts. After that, I planned the homepage structure, reviewed the visual direction, and identified improvements through a curator-style review.

This process helped me stay organized, reduce drift, and keep the site aligned with the project goals.

## Curator Review Approach
I reviewed the site as if it were a curated exhibition rather than just a normal website.

The review focused on:
- narrative flow
- exhibit organization
- artifact presentation
- educational value
- emotional engagement
- visual coherence
- visitor experience

## Current Improvement Goals
The current improvement goals for the project are:
- create a stronger first impression in the hero section
- use more striking visuals and faces to create emotional connection
- make the site feel more immersive and museum-like
- improve visual consistency across sections
- strengthen the overall sense of originality and exhibition design
